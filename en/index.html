Here is what we offer

For companies we produce large and small series

Apart from precise design, we also offer competent advice and individual service to our customers. For large series, we first create a prototype. If this is in accordance with your ideas, the order will go into production. We are also happy to arrange framework agreements with our customers and usually sign them for 12 to 24 months.

  • Turning

  • Milling

  • Measure

  • Assembly

  • Welding

On our CNC rotation machines, we process prototypes and serial parts. Rotating parts up to Ø 300 mm belong to our machining range. All machines are equipped with short-loading magazines.

Our state-of-the-art machines leave nothing to be desired. We manufacture for you from item to large series keeping the same high quality. We also produce new products at night. (Indunorm facility).

Thanks to state-of-the-art measuring instruments, we guarantee the highest quality on request even with test protocol.

We would be happy to assemble your modules in our company and thus guarantee you the shortest delivery times from our warehouse. Just ask us!

Steel, stainless steel and aluminum are our standard materials, from the simple stitching to the elaborate welding design. This way you always get a solid and cleanly processed product of tested quality.
